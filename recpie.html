Healthy Recipes

Chickpea Salad with Cucumber & Carrots

Calories: 328 calories

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup boiled chickpeas: 164 calories
  • 1 medium cucumber: 16 calories
  • 1 medium carrot: 25 calories
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil: 119 calories
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice: 4 calories
  • Salt: to taste
  • Black pepper: to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil Chickpeas: Soak 1 cup dried chickpeas overnight, then boil until tender (about 1-1.5 hours) or use canned chickpeas.
  2. Prepare Veggies: Wash, peel, and chop 1 cucumber and 1 carrot into small chunks.
  3. Make Dressing: Mix 1 tablespoon olive oil, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, salt, and black pepper in a small bowl.
  4. Combine: In a large bowl, mix boiled chickpeas, cucumber, and carrot. Pour the dressing over and toss well.
  5. Serve: Adjust seasoning to taste and enjoy fresh!

Rajma & Vegetable Pulao

Calories: 400 kcal

Ingredients:

  • 1 chicken breast
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup carrot slices
  • 1 cup sliced bell peppers

Instructions:

  1. Preheat grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Brush chicken with olive oil and season with garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  3. Grill chicken for 6-8 minutes on each side.
  4. Steam vegetables for 5-7 minutes and serve.

Almond Butter and Raspberry Smoothie Bowl

Calories (without toppings): 283 calories

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup frozen raspberries: ~65 calories
  • 1 medium banana: ~105 calories
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter: ~98 calories
  • 1/2 cup almond milk (unsweetened): ~15 calories
  • Toppings: sliced fruits, granola, chia seeds

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, combine 1 cup frozen raspberries, 1 medium banana, 1 tablespoon almond butter, and 1/2 cup almond milk. Blend until smooth add honey or maple syrup if you prefer a sweeter flavor.
  2. Pour into a bowl and add your desired toppings.

Avocado Toast

Calories: 250 kcal

Ingredients:

  • 1 slice whole grain bread
  • 1/2 ripe avocado
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • Red pepper flakes (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Toast the bread to your liking.
  2. Mash avocado and spread over toast.
  3. Drizzle with olive oil and season with salt, pepper, and red pepper flakes.

Oatmeal with Berries

Calories: 300 kcal

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1 cup water or milk
  • 1/4 cup mixed berries
  • 1 tbsp honey or maple syrup

Instructions:

  1. In a saucepan, bring oats and water (or milk) to a boil.
  2. Simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Top with berries and drizzle with honey or maple syrup.

Vegetable Stir-fry

Calories: 320 kcal

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 cup broccoli florets
  • 1/2 cup bell peppers, sliced
  • 1/2 cup carrot slices
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 garlic clove, minced

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add garlic and stir for 30 seconds.
  3. Add vegetables and stir-fry for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Stir in soy sauce and serve immediately.

Grilled Lemon Herb Chicken

Calories: ~220 calories (per 4 oz serving)

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz chicken breast
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon dried herbs (oregano, thyme, or basil)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken with olive oil, lemon juice, herbs, salt, and pepper for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat grill and cook chicken for 6-7 minutes on each side until cooked through.
  3. Let rest, slice, and serve.

Baked Salmon with Asparagus

Calories: ~350 calories (for 4 oz salmon with 1 cup asparagus)

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz salmon fillet
  • 1 cup asparagus
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss asparagus with olive oil, salt, and pepper; place on a baking sheet.
  3. Place salmon on the sheet, season, and top with lemon slices.
  4. Bake for 12-15 minutes until salmon is cooked through.

Shrimp Stir-Fry

Calories:~250 calories (for 4 oz shrimp with veggies)

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, carrots)
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan, add shrimp and cook until pink.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  3. Add soy sauce and cook for another minute.

Chicken Tikka Salad

Calories: ~350 calories (for 4 oz chicken with salad)

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz chicken breast, marinated in yogurt and spices
  • Mixed salad greens
  • 1/4 cucumber, sliced
  • 1/4 red onion, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon vinaigrette

Instructions:

  1. Grill or bake marinated chicken until cooked through, then slice.
  2. Toss salad greens, cucumber, onion, and vinaigrette in a bowl.
  3. Top with sliced chicken and serve.

Egg and Spinach Breakfast Bowl

Calories: ~290 calories (for 2 eggs and veggies)

Ingredients:

  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup fresh spinach
  • 1/4 avocado
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Sauté spinach in a non-stick pan until wilted.
  2. Scramble or fry eggs in the same pan, season with salt and pepper.
  3. Serve eggs over spinach, topped with sliced avocado.

Lemon Garlic Shrimp with Broccoli

Calories:~280 calories (for 4 oz shrimp and 1 cup broccoli)

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Lemon juice, salt, and pepper

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan, add garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  2. Add shrimp and broccoli, cooking until shrimp are pink and broccoli is tender.
  3. Finish with lemon juice, salt, and pepper before serving.
