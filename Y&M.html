Yoga and Meditation


YOGA POSES

Downward-Facing Dog

Procedure:

  • Start on your hands and knees.
  • Lift your hips up and back.
  • Press your heels toward the floor.

Benefits:

  • Strengthens arms and legs.
  • Improves blood circulation.
  • Relieves tension.

Warrior II

Procedure:

  • Stand with your feet apart.
  • Bend your right knee over your right ankle.

Benefits:

  • Builds strength and stamina.
  • Enhances focus and balance.
  • Bend your right knee over your right ankle, keeping your left leg straight.

Child’s Pose

Procedure:

  • Kneel and sit back on your heels.
  • Extend your arms forward and rest your forehead on the mat.

Benefits:

  • Relaxes the body and calms the mind.
  • Stretches the back and hips.
  • Reduces stress and fatigue.

Cobra Pose

Procedure:

  • Lie face down and place your hands under your shoulders.
  • Press into your hands to lift your chest off the ground.

Benefits:

  • Strengthens the spine and opens the chest.
  • Improves digestion.

Tree Pose

Procedure:

  • Stand tall and shift weight onto one foot.
  • Place the other foot on your inner thigh or calf.
  • Bring palms together in front of your heart.

Benefits:

  • Improves balance and stability.

MEDITATION TECHNIQUES

Mindfulness Meditation

Procedure:

  • Find a comfortable seated position.
  • Focus on your breath.
  • Gently bring your focus back when your mind wanders.

Benefits:

  • Reduces stress and anxiety.
  • Increases self-awareness.
Guided Visualization

Procedure:

  • Sit or lie down comfortably.
  • Imagine a peaceful scene and engage your senses.

Benefits:

  • Promotes relaxation and reduces stress.
Loving-Kindness Meditation

Procedure:

  • Close your eyes and focus on your heart area.
  • Silently repeat phrases of goodwill for yourself and others.

Benefits:

  • Increases compassion and empathy.
  • Reduces negative emotions.
